About Clarise

Architect · Designer

Clarise M.
Lutiva

BS Architecture · Quezon City, Philippines

Clarise M. Lutiva is an emerging architectural designer based in the Philippines, with a comprehensive portfolio spanning urban commercial development, hospitality, landscape, interior design, and multimodal street planning.

Her academic journey — documented through both digital and manual works from 2018 to 2025 — reflects a disciplined approach to design thinking, anchored in context, heritage, and human experience. From hand-drawn cathedral renderings to thesis-level urban frameworks, Clarise brings both craft and vision to every project.

"Architecture is not just about shelter — it is about shaping how people move, gather, feel, and belong within a space."
